In the shadows of a mysterious forest, where the lines between devotion and darkness blur, lies a culinary secret known only to a select few. Inspired by the twisted world of Cult of the Lamb, we bring you a dish that will test your resolve and tantalize your taste buds: Minced Follower Meat.

Ingredients

  • 3 Follower Meat (Harvested from deceased followers or obtained through sacrifice rituals. Don't worry, they won't be needing it anymore.)
  • 5 Bones (Collected from defeated enemies or follower sacrifices. Bonus points if they're still warm.)

Instructions

  1. Chop the Follower Meat: Finely chop the follower meat into small, uniform pieces to ensure even cooking.
  2. Crush the Bones: Use a mortar and pestle to crush the bones into a coarse powder. This will add texture and a hint of darkness to the dish.
  3. Mix the Ingredients: Combine the chopped follower meat and crushed bones in a large bowl. Mix well to distribute the ingredients evenly.
    4. Cooking Method: Place the mixture in a cooking pot over medium heat. Stir occasionally until the mixture is heated through. Be cautious, as this dish can be volatile.
  4. Serve: Serve hot to your followers. Be prepared for potential side effects, including illness and increased loyalty.

Tips

  • Consider giving your followers the Cannibal Trait to mitigate negative effects and gain faith.
  • Use this dish strategically to manage follower behavior and loyalty.
